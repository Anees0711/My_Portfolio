LSTM-Based Remaining Useful Life Prediction
EPITA MSc Thesis (Python · TensorFlow · FastAPI · Streamlit)
Deep Learning · Time-Series

The challenge was predicting turbofan engine failure from noisy multi-sensor time-series using the NASA C-MAPSS benchmark. I designed LSTM networks with sliding-window generation, dropout, and custom loss functions and benchmarked them against Random Forest, Linear Regression, and SVR baselines. The LSTM achieved R² ≈ 0.87 on held-out data and was deployed as a FastAPI and Streamlit real-time inference prototype.
